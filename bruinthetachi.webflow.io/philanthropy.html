WHAT MAKES THETA CHI UNIQUE

Theta Chi stands out for its strong commitment to the principles of Truth, Temperance, and Tolerance, creating a supportive brotherhood focused on personal growth, academic excellence, leadership development, and community service.

We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive community

HOW DOES THETA CHI 
Connect WITH ALUMNI

Theta Chi maintains strong ties with its alumni through networking events, reunions, and mentorship programs, offering valuable opportunities for career guidance and support.

These connections with alumni foster a sense of brotherhood that extends far beyond college, enriching the Theta Chi experience for a lifetime.

How does Theta Chi promote a safe
SOCIAL culture?

Theta Chi prioritizes a safe social culture by adhering to strict policies, promoting alcohol education, and ensuring a safe environment at all fraternity events.

By promoting a responsible social culture, Theta Chi ensures that members and guests can enjoy social events in a safe and supportive atmosphere, fostering memorable experiences while upholding the fraternity's values.

How does Theta Chi give back to the community

Theta Chi participates in various community service projects, charity fundraisers, and philanthropic events to positively impact the local community and beyond.
‍
As committed stewards of community engagement, Theta Chi takes pride in being the number one donor for Relay for Life each year, exemplifying our dedication to making a significant difference in the fight against cancer.

What role does athletics and sports play in Theta Chi?

Theta Chi encourages participation in intramural sports, promoting teamwork, physical fitness, and friendly competition among its members.
‍
Through intramural sports, Theta Chi fosters a vibrant spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition, strengthening the bonds between brothers and promoting a well-rounded lifestyle.
